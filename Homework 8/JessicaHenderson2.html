I am a writer, here is a poem:

Crying Girl at Midnight

the crying girl at midnight
looked up at the moon
with sparkles in her eyes
and wished for her love
she wished on every star
but had never gone as far
to ask the moon
to give her true love
she prayed and prayed
for the pain to go away
the moon
only turned his face away
now the crying girl at midnight
will cry all day
